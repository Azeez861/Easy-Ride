Common Driver Questions

Can I become an Easy Ride driver in my city?

If Easy Ride operates in your city, you can apply to become a driver. Once your application is reviewed and approved, you'll be able to start accepting rides and earning money.

How can I become an Easy Ride driver?

Sign up for an Easy Ride driver account, upload the required documents, complete the verification process, and wait for approval. Once your account is activated, you can start accepting rides and earning.

Do I need to own a car to drive with Easy Ride?

Easy Ride partners with vehicle owners who may rent their cars to eligible drivers, making it possible to start driving even if you don't own a vehicle.

What are the requirements for the smartphone?

To drive with Easy Ride, you'll need a smartphone with a stable internet connection that can run the Easy Ride Driver app. Android and iPhone devices with GPS enabled are supported for receiving ride requests, navigation, and managing your trips.

When will I receive my earnings?

Your earnings are transferred to your registered bank account according to Easy Ride's payout schedule.
